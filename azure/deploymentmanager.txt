
Group
    az deploymentmanager : Create and manage rollouts for your service.
        To deploy your service across many regions and make sure it is running as expected in each
        region, you can use deployment manager to coordinate a staged rollout of the service. For
        more details, visit https://docs.microsoft.com/azure/azure-resource-manager/deployment-
        manager-overview.
        WARNING: This command group is in preview and under development. Reference and support
        levels: https://aka.ms/CLI_refstatus

Subgroups:
    artifact-source  : Manage artifact sources.
    rollout          : Manage the rollouts.
    service          : Manage the services in a service topology.
    service-topology : Manage service topologies.
    service-unit     : Manage the service units.
    step             : Manage the steps.

To search AI knowledge base for examples, use: az find "az deploymentmanager"
