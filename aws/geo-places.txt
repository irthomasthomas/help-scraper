NAME
       geo-places -

DESCRIPTION
       The Places API enables powerful location search and geocoding capabili-
       ties for your applications, offering global  coverage  with  rich,  de-
       tailed information. Key features include:

       o Forward and reverse geocoding for addresses and coordinates

       o Comprehensive place searches with detailed information, including:

         o Business names and addresses

         o Contact information

         o Hours of operation

         o POI (Points of Interest) categories

         o Food types for restaurants

         o Chain affiliation for relevant businesses

       o Global data coverage with a wide range of POI categories

       o Regular data updates to ensure accuracy and relevance

AVAILABLE COMMANDS
       o autocomplete

       o geocode

       o get-place

       o help

       o reverse-geocode

       o search-nearby

       o search-text

       o suggest
