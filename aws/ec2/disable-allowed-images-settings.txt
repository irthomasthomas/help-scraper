NAME
       disable-allowed-images-settings -

DESCRIPTION
       Disables Allowed AMIs for your account in the specified Amazon Web Ser-
       vices Region. When set to disabled , the image criteria in your Allowed
       AMIs  settings do not apply, and no restrictions are placed on AMI dis-
       coverability or usage. Users in your account can launch instances using
       any public AMI or AMI shared with your account.

       NOTE:
          The  Allowed  AMIs  feature does not restrict the AMIs owned by your
          account. Regardless of the criteria you set,  the  AMIs  created  by
          your account will always be discoverable and usable by users in your
          account.

       For more information, see Control the discovery and use of AMIs in Ama-
       zon EC2 with Allowed AMIs in Amazon EC2 User Guide .

       See also: AWS API Documentation
